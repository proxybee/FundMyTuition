FundMyTuition Process

Have an idea but confused and do not know exactly where to satrt from?
Go through the following list of processes we have put together to guide you

On FundMyTuition you can campaign for funding for any of the following: Tuition Fund, Student Loan payment, Research, Edu-projects, Institution, Mentorship, skill acquisition or any activity that promotes educational innovation

Educational Financial Need

You have a financial need for your tuition, student loan or project, perherps you came up with a great research idea as a result of which the universe could get totally transformed or you just want to mentor/enable a group of students, wow a mazing but then what next.

Register on FundMyTuition

FundMyTuitiion can be utilized to source for all your financial needs, but you will need tp satrt from registering on the platform, even if you are not ready to proceed with your campaign, make the first neccessary move and register here. We recommend you find out what is required of your choise of campaign before going ahead with creating the project.

Create your Campaign

When you are absolutely ready and have all that is required of you and very convinced you can put in the work required to meet your goal. You can then go ahead and forward your campaign to FundMyTuition for review and approval. The review shouldn't take long if you have filled your form correctly and provided all that is required.You will be notiffied when your project go live.

To Achieve Your Goal

Use every possible available means at your disposal and remember the easiest way to get attention is being creative and differently, if you campaigning like everyother person you get results like every other person.

Promote your Campaign

Make sure you use all available platform to promote your campaign, remember it is either 100% or no funding, this is solely to ensure 100% seriousness, to sift out the shaft from the grains. so ensure only ask for fund you can achieve 100%, to do this you can ask for your fund in bits one request at a time, ensure you attain one goal before moving to the next fund request goal you use every platform available to you

Campaign Visibility

Your family and friends matters a lot they are the first to fund and help promote your campaign to give your campaign an edge on the platform and make it more vissible. If you cannot get your family and friends to fund you how much more a stranger. The platform is designed to make more engaging and fast funded campaigns more vissible

Withdraw your Funds

And at the end of your hard work pays, you can withdraw your money, fund your project, tuition or research etc. and do not forget to thank your funders
