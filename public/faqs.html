FundMyTuition Basics

1. Question: What is FundMyTuition?

Ans:FundMyTuition is a global public benefit Organization, focused on education, intended run in major zonal regions across the globe. Our mission is to reduce the level of anxiety and hassles students face in sourcing for their tuition or paying off their loans as well as aid quality and innovative Education/Research
You can source for funds for any of the following on FundMyTuition

  • Tuition
  • Student Loan
  • Edu-projects
  • Edu-Events
  • Research
  • And any other activity that can contribute innovatively to enhancing our educational system

2. Question: Why FundMyTuition?

Ans: Here everyone have an equal opportunity at learning and meeting their career goals, you no longer have an excuse to why you shouldn't learn that skill you are passionate about and why you should not succeed in life, you just need to come out of your comfort zone, make the move, do a little campaign here and there and bam! that dream you always had is your reality.
More interestingly, you do not need to break a bank or collect a student that would linger for life to do that.

We encourage team campaign, you see easy it is to break a stick but hard to break a tree, well we simply believe two good heads are better than one, hence we simply encourage getting people you trust in your team to help you in your campaign

At FundMyTuition it is 100% goal or no goal, we do not believe you should raise just half Tuition, project funding, or student loan it is either we are meeting your educational/research funding need wholly or we are not meeting it at all, hence we encourage you to either split your funding goals or subtract the amount you know you would be getting from other source of funding e.g Scholarship, sponsorship and all, before creating your target, always put up your minimum required funding or you double up your campaign efforts to meet your target. Ensure you are equal to the task before you insert your target.

3. Question: Does this mean I can only raise Team Funding?

Ans: No, absolutely not, the fact that you invite a team to join your campaign doesn't mean the team owns the funding. they are simply a set of people who believe in your goals and want to see you win.

4. Question: How much can I raise with FundMyTuition?

Ans: You can raise any amount you require for your Educational use as long as you are capable of raising 100% of your fund target.

5. Question:What do you mean by 100% goal or no goal?

Ans: It means you have to successfully raise 100% of your minimum required funding to enable you process the receipt of your campaign funds.

Reward (perks) Tips

1. Question: What are perks?

Ans: Perks is just another name for rewards or attraction or bonuses it is kind of an enticement.

2. Question: Are perks necessary?

Ans: Not exactly but it is a good side attraction especially if funders are personally interested in your choice of campaign and would like to be a part of the end result. Infact studies have shown that campaigns with perks do 80% better those without.

3. Question: What else do I need to know about perks?

Ans:We always advice against getting expensive perks or choosing a perk that would require shipment, still you need to be aware of the tips listed;

  • Ensure you factor in the price of your perks and shipment into your targeted amount
  • Make sure it is not very expensive but it is valuable, some funders just really want to be updated on your success they are not interested in your reward or perks or whatever
  • Ensure it is relative to the campaign e.g. a postcard, acknowledgement on all your platform, your transcript or result, a video, your research findings, special invitation to unveiling of the research, project or graduation in question etc. You can adopt the above and many more and less stressful perks
  • Be creative with your perks, you do not have to stick to the normal choice of perks you see around
  • Always remember that your perks is not in exchange for the donations and it can never pay back the funder in full, hence always be humble, grateful and polite to your funders
